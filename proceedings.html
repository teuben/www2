Conference Proceedings

Order Hardcopies from ASP

The proceedings for each of the ADASS conferences have been published in hardcopy form by the Astronomical Society of the Pacific. Hardcopy volumes of the ASP Conference Series may be ordered from ASP. When possible, and in cooperation with ASP, the proceedings are also offered on-line from this site. Future editions will be made freely and electronically available directly from ASP.
